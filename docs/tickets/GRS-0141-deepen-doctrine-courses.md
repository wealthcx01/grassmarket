# GRS-0141 — Deepen the doctrine course bodies (show, don't just tell)

**Status:** In progress
**Loop:** Part 2 — critical review / trust hardening

## Why

The critical review found the mandatory doctrine courses were the thinnest content in the catalog:
one abstract paragraph per lesson that *told* the principle but never *showed* it — "no example
script, no sample dialogue, no worked teach-insight". For the courses a new advisor takes FIRST, that
was the weakest learning. This deepens the bodies to teach the how, not only the what.

## What changed

Every lesson in the two doctrine courses now follows a **principle → In practice → The move** shape:

- **Sales Egoist** (8 lessons) — each body grew from ~90 to ~205 words with a concrete worked example
  and **sample dialogue** grounded in a real operating-model scenario, then a specific action. E.g.
  the Zero-Sum Pipeline lesson now shows the exact line an Egoist uses to re-open a gone-quiet wealth
  prospect; Total Account Awareness walks a real four-person retail-brokerage buying unit; the
  Challenger–Teach lesson shows a concrete "your scale is your exposure, not your moat" reframe.
- **Sales Operations Playbook** (4 lessons) — each stage lesson (~90 → ~210 words) now walks a
  concrete account through that pipeline stage with a sample line and the exact CRM move, and keeps
  the v7 commission mechanics grounded (self-sourced vs firm-sourced, Stream A/B, the recovery fee).

No structure change: bodies are markdown paragraphs the reader already renders (blank-line separated,
inline **bold**). No contract/schema/frontend change. The comprehension checks + auto-enrolled drills
(GRS-0139) are unchanged and still fire.

## Flagged (still open)
- Deepen the product-course *bodies* similarly if wanted (they are already deep and research-grounded).
- The comprehension gate is still UX-level active recall (not a server-enforced quiz); tie cert
  sign-off to a drill/arena record.

## Acceptance
- Each doctrine lesson has a worked example / sample dialogue and a concrete move, ~200+ words; the
  courses build and all course/academy tests pass; the reader renders the paragraphs + bold. Golden
  master untouched.
