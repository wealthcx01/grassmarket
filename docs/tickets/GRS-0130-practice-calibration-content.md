# GRS-0130 — Feed practice arena + calibration from the courses

**Status:** Planned
**Loop:** Part 2 — Bruntsfield Academy / Workbench (one program)
**Depends on:** ADR-0028 (Bruntsfield Academy / Workbench)

## Why

The practice arena and calibration sessions should let advisers rehearse the **real** motions, which
means drawing on the Sales Egoist doctrine and the product courses rather than generic scenarios.
Calibration is also under-explained — the primer/guide never says what a calibration session is. This
ticket connects the Academy content into practice/calibration and documents calibration.

## What to build

- Feed **practice-arena scenarios** and **calibration sessions** from Sales Egoist (GRS-0122) + product
  content (GRS-0123–0126) so advisers rehearse the actual sales/assessment motions. Files: `workbench/`
  practice + calibration.
- **Define what calibration sessions are** in the primer/guide.
- Keep practice-arena feedback **self-scoped and AI-labelled** — not an approval record — per CLAUDE.md
  non-negotiable #8 (its gate is the AI-drafted label + self-scoping, deliberately not a recorded
  approval).

## Acceptance / verification

- Practice-arena scenarios and calibration sessions reference Sales Egoist + product course content, not
  generic filler.
- The primer/guide explains what a calibration session is.
- Practice-arena feedback remains self-scoped and AI-labelled with no approval record (non-negotiable #8
  preserved).

## Not in scope

- The course content itself (GRS-0122–0126).
- Committee/dual-rating governance (that stays approval-recorded; only practice feedback is self-scoped).
